What is Saralta Darshan?

A philosophy created for people who feel overwhelmed by the complexity of life.

Saralta Darshan is a philosophy created for people who feel overwhelmed by the complexity of life.

It does not claim ultimate truth.

It does not oppose any religion, ideology or belief system.

Its purpose is to provide a simple, practical and peaceful framework for living.

The philosophy is built upon three core principles:

  • Humanity
  • Ahimsa
  • Happiness

Every important decision in life can be evaluated through these principles.

Core Principles

Principles that guide thoughtful living.

Humanity

Humanity is the highest moral principle. Truth, honesty, compassion and respect shall guide human interactions. Deception, betrayal and unnecessary emotional harm are considered contrary to Humanity.

Ahimsa

Ahimsa represents respect for life. Unnecessary violence and harm should be avoided whenever possible. Life should be treated with dignity and respect.

Happiness

Happiness is an essential part of life. Mental peace, emotional wellbeing and long-term fulfillment should be protected. No person should remain trapped in endless suffering.

The Invisible Force

Saralta Darshan maintains faith in an Invisible Force.

Its nature is unknown. Arguments regarding its exact form are not considered important. Faith is encouraged. Expectation is not.

Faith shall be maintained. Expectations shall not.
